About Washington

Washington State is famous for its beautiful landscapes. From the tall mountains and green forests to the clear coastlines, there's something for everyone. The state has many parks, wildlife, and different types of weather, from rainy forests to dry deserts. Cities like Seattle are known for their technology, including companies like Microsoft and Amazon. Whether you love nature or the city life, Washington has it all.

facts

  1. Washington is home to Mount Rainier, the highest peak in the state.

  2. Seattle is known for being a big tech city with companies like Microsoft and Amazon.

  3. The Columbia River Gorge is famous for its beautiful waterfalls.

  4. Washington is the top producer of apples in the U.S. and has great wine.
