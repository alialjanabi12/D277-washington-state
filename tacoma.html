Discover the Beauty of Tacoma

Tacoma is a lively city with a beautiful waterfront, creative art scene, and rich history. Enjoy its mix of nature, culture, and fun activities.

place I

01 Dale Chihuly's Glass Art

The Chihuly Bridge of Glass is a 500-foot walkway filled with stunning glass sculptures. It connects the Museum of Glass to the Washington State History Museum, making it a free outdoor art display.

Photo by Hoag Levi
Photo by Aaron Locke

place II

02 Pantages Theater

The Pantages Theater opened in 1918 and was restored in 2018. It is one of Tacoma’s most famous landmarks, offering plays, concerts, and shows in a beautiful, historic building.

place III

03Mount Rainier

Mount Rainier, also called Tahoma, is a giant volcano near Tacoma. It’s famous for its tall peaks, hiking trails, and amazing views, perfect for nature lovers and adventurers.
