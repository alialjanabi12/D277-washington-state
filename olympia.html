Discover the Beauty of olympia

Olympia, the capital of Washington State, is known for its stunning landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture. Visitors can explore historic sites, enjoy beautiful parks, and experience local arts and food scenes. Whether you're interested in nature, history, or shopping, Olympia has something for everyone.

place I

01 Washington State Capital

The Washington State Capitol is home to beautiful historic buildings and monuments. Explore the grand Legislative Building, the Governor's Mansion, and the Temple of Justice. Visitors can take guided or self-guided tours to learn about the state’s history while enjoying unique art pieces and memorials throughout the campus.

place II

02 Capital Mall

Capital Mall is the perfect spot for shopping, dining, and entertainment. With over 100 stores, restaurants, and a 14-screen theater, there's something for everyone. The mall also supports local artists and small businesses, making it a vibrant place to visit.

place III

03 Puget Sound Estuarium

The Puget Sound Estuarium is a small aquarium showcasing marine life where freshwater and saltwater meet. See sea stars, anemones, and other fascinating creatures up close. Join guided programs like Pier Peer or Meet the Beach for a hands-on experience with marine habitats.
