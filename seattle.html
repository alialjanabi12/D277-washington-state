Discover the Beauty of Seattle

Seattle is a city full of exciting places to visit, from its iconic landmarks to its vibrant culture. Let’s explore some of its most famous attractions.

place I

01 Space Needle

The Space Needle, built for the 1962 World’s Fair, is a famous Seattle landmark standing 605 feet tall. You can ride an elevator to the top in just 43 seconds and enjoy amazing views of the city, mountains, and water.

Photo by Matthew Johnson
Photo by Josh Hild

place II

02 Pike Place Market

Pike Place Market is one of Seattle's most popular places, filled with shops, restaurants, and street performers. It’s famous for its fresh seafood, flowers, and the first Starbucks store.

place III

03 Central Public Library

Seattle's Central Public Library is a modern, glass-covered building designed by architect Rem Koolhaas. It’s a great place to read, study, and explore its large collection of books, movies, and more.
